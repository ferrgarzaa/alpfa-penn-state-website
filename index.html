Who We Are

ALPFA at Penn State

The Association of Latino Professionals For America (ALPFA) is the nation's largest Latino professional association. Our Penn State chapter, housed within the Smeal College of Business, empowers Latino and allied students to become leaders in business, industry, and their communities through professional development, mentorship, and authentic networking. We are open to all majors — from Smeal to Engineering, IST, and beyond.
