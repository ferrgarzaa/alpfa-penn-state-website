What makes the Masquerade unique is its format. During the dinner and networking portion of the event, all attendees — including recruiters and company representatives — wear masks. This encourages open, authentic, and unbiased conversation between students and professionals before identities are known. After the reveal, the event transitions into a standard career-fair-style networking session.

How the Evening Unfolds

01
Masked Dinner

Companies are known to attendees, but individual identities stay hidden.

02
Grand Reveal

Masks come off at the conclusion of dinner.

03
Career Fair Networking

Open networking session, one-on-one with recruiters.

Who Attends

  • Students across Accounting, Finance, Management, Marketing, Business Analytics, Supply Chain, and more.
  • Recruiters and representatives from our corporate partners.
  • ALPFA Penn State members and allied student organizations.
