Flagship Event

The Masquerade Ball 2026

A different kind of networking evening. The Masquerade is a formal dinner and networking event centered on diversity leadership — our chapter's biggest event of the year.

Masquerade Ball

📅 September 30, 2026 🕑 4:00 PM – 9:00 PM 📍 Smeal College of Business Atrium, 220 Business Building

What makes the Masquerade unique is its format. During the dinner and networking portion of the event, all attendees — including recruiters and company representatives — wear masks. This encourages open, authentic, and unbiased conversation between students and professionals before identities are known. Attendees are asked to avoid clothing or accessories with visible company logos during the masked portion. After the reveal, the event transitions into a standard career-fair-style networking session.

How the Evening Unfolds

01
Masked Dinner

Companies are known to attendees, but individual identities stay hidden.

02
Grand Reveal

Masks come off at the conclusion of dinner.

03
Career Fair

Open networking session, one-on-one with recruiters.

Who Attends

  • Students across Accounting, Finance, Management, Marketing, Business Analytics, Supply Chain Management, Data Analysis, and more.
  • Recruiters and representatives from our corporate partners.
  • ALPFA Penn State members and allied student organizations.
